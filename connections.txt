This file descriptions the wiring between the Pi and the Pico for the antenna code

Pi 		    Pico
------------------------------------
PIN 33 (GPIO 13) -> PIN 6  (GPIO 4)
PIN 35 (GPIO 19) -> PIN 7  (GPIO 5)
PIN 37 (GPIO 26) -> PIN 10 (GPIO 7)
PIN 8 (UART0 TX) -> PIN 9 (UART1 RX)
PIN 10 (UART0 RX)-> PIN 8 (UART1 TX)
